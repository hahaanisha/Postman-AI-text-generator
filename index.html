AI Text Summarizer App By Anisha

Welcome to the AI Text Summarizer App by Anisha! As part of Postman's course, this app utilizes the power of Hugging Face APIs, specifically the facebook/bart-large-cnn model, to generate concise summaries of long texts. Whether you have a lengthy article, research paper, or any other text document that you need summarized quickly, our app is here to help.

Simply paste your text into the text area below and click the Submit button.
